The Tempered Broadsword is a sword weapon that can be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on levels 40+ in the Skull Cavern. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 90 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 8 to 7 and sell price lowered from 400g to 350g.  Bug where weapon defense had no effect fixed.
