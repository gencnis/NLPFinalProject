Ice Cream is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit. 
Ice Cream can be purchased from Alex during Summer at the Ice Cream Stand for 250g, or at the Oasis on Sundays (year-round) for 240g. It may also randomly appear in Krobus' shop on Saturdays or in the Stardrop Saloon's rotating stock.
