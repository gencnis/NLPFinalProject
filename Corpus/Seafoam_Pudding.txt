Seafoam Pudding is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit. One can be purchased for 5,000g from the shop at the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies festival each year. It may also randomly appear at the Traveling Cart for 900-1,500g.

Tailoring
Seafoam Pudding is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Mineral Dog Jacket.  It can be used in dyeing, serving as a blue dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
