Dwarf Gadget





It's a piece of the advanced technology once known to the dwarves. It's still glowing and humming, but you're unable to understand how it works.

The Dwarf Gadget is an Artifact that can be found by digging up soil in the Mines (floors 41-80).  It can also be found in Magma Geodes, Omni Geodes, and Artifact Troves.

Donation
Donation of this item contributes to the total count of donations at the Museum.  To earn the 'Burnt Offering' reward, you must donate a Rare Disc, a Dwarf Gadget, and 11+ artifacts.
