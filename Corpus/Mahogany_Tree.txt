A Mahogany Tree is a common tree that grows from a Mahogany Seed. It yields Sap every day when tapped.  It can be chopped down with an axe, producing 8-13 Hardwood.[1]
Unlike other fertilized tree seeds that have a 100% chance of growing to the next stage, Mahogany Seeds fertilized with Tree Fertilizer have a 60% chance of growing to the next stage each night. Unfertilized seeds have a 15% chance of growing to the next stage.
