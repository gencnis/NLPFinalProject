Mushroom Tree Seed





Place this on your farm to plant a mushroom tree.


Information 


Source:

Qi's Walnut Room for  5


Growth Time:

Variable


Season:

 All


Sell Price:

Cannot be sold


The Mushroom Tree Seed is a seed that grows into a Mushroom Tree.  It's purchasable from Mr. Qi's shop in  Qi's Walnut Room, for  5 Qi Gems.

Tailoring
Mushroom Tree Seed can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the  Star Helmet It can also serve as a red dye color in the sewing machine or dye pots, located at Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
