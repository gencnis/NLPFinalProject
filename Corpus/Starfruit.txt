Tailoring
Starfruit can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Genie Pants.  It can be used in dyeing, serving as a yellow dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests
Kent requests a Starfruit by mail on the 15th of Summer (year 2) in the "A Soldier's Star" Quest.  The reward is 500g and 1 Friendship heart.
Trivia
The Starfruit's sprite depicts a cross-sectional slice of a real starfruit, which is oblong in shape.
1 Starfruit can be purchased each year at the Summer Luau for 3,000g.
