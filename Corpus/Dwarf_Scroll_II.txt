Dwarf Scroll II





A yellowed scroll of parchment filled with dwarven script. This one's tied with a green ribbon.

The Dwarf Scroll II is an Artifact that can be found by tilling soil in The Mines (floors 1-40, 0.1% chance), as a drop from a Frost Bat (floors 40-79), as a drop from a Dust Sprite (floors 40-79), or as a drop from Blue Slimes or Ghosts.

Tailoring
Dwarf Scroll II can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Gold Trimmed Shirt.   It can be used in dyeing, serving as a green dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests
One Dwarf Scroll II may be requested by Ghostfish in a Fish Pond quest to increase the capacity of the pond from 7 to 10.
