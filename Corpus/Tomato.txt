Tailoring
Tomato is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Tomato Shirt.    It can also be used as a red dye in the dye pots, located at Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
The Tomato Shirt is seemingly a Mix Between Robin and Demetrius' shirts, with the left side being Robin and the right side being Demetrius.

Quests
The Tomato may be randomly requested in Summer at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 180g and 150 Friendship points.

Trivia
The ambiguous status of the tomato - fruit or vegetable - is the subject of Demetrius' Six Hearts event.

History
