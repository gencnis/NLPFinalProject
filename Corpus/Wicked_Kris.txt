The Wicked Kris is a dagger weapon that can be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on levels 40+ in the Skull Cavern.  It can also be dropped by Haunted Skulls, in the Quarry Mine or in dungeon levels in the Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 90 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 15 to 8 and sell price lowered from 750g to 400g.
