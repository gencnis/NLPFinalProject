Shadow Snipers are an enemy found in the Mines after activating the Shrine of Challenge or during the Danger In The Deep quest.
It is the difficult counterpart of the Shadow Brute.

Behavior
Shoots at the player from a distance and inflicts the  Darkness debuff on hit. Their projectiles can be dodged by approaching them from an angle, due to to their limitations to only being able to shoot in a straight line.

History
1.5: Introduced.
