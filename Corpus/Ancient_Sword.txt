Ancient Sword





It's the remains of an ancient sword. Most of the blade has turned to rust, but the hilt is very finely crafted.


Information


Artifact Spots:

Cindersap Forest (0.7%)The Mountain (0.6%)


Monster Drops:

N/A


Other Sources:

 Fishing Treasure Chest Artifact Trove


Sell Price:

100g

The Ancient Sword is an Artifact that can be found by digging up an Artifact Spot in Cindersap Forest or the Mountains, and from Fishing Treasure Chests or Artifact Troves.

Bundles
Ancient Sword is not used in any bundles.

Recipes
Ancient Sword is not used in any recipes.

Tailoring
Ancient Sword is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth in the feed to create a  Green Tunic. It is a gray dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed.

Quests
Ancient Sword is not used in any quests.

Trivia
The green tunic tailored from ancient sword and cloth is similar to the green tunic worn by Link in The Legend of Zelda series of video games.
