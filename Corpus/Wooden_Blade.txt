The Wooden Blade is a sword weapon that can be obtained from the Adventurer's Guild for 250g as soon as the Adventurer's Guild is unlocked. It is also a possible reward for the chest on level 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 2 to 1 and sell price lowered from 100g to 50g. Added as a possible reward on floor 10 in the remixed Mines.
