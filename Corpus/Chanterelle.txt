Tailoring
Chanterelle is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth in the feed to create an  Orange Shirt. It is a brown dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed. It can be placed in the orange dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house for use in dyeing.

Quests
Chanterelle is not used in any quests.
