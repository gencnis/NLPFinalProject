A Bat Wing is a type of Monster Loot dropped by Bats (any variant except for Iridium Bats) when slain. 10 bat wings can also be purchased for 30g each from Krobus on Sundays.
The Desert Trader in the Calico Desert will trade one Butterfly Hutch for 200 Bat Wings.
