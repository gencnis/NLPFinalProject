Dwarvish Helm





It's one of the helmets commonly worn by dwarves. The thick metal plating protects them from falling debris and stalactites.

The Dwarvish Helm is an Artifact that can be found by tilling soil in The Mines (level 1-40), or found inside Geodes, Omni Geodes, or Artifact Troves.

Recipes

Tailoring
The Dwarvish Helm can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the  Wearable Dwarf Helm. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

The Dwarvish Helm is not used in any quests.
