Limestone





A very common type of stone. It's not worth very much.


Information


Source:

 Geode  Omni Geode  Coral (Fish Pond)


Sell Price:

15g


 Gemologist Profession: (+30% Sell Price)

19g

Limestone is a mineral that can be found in the Geode and the Omni Geode.  A Coral Fish Pond may produce 1 Limestone when the population of the pond reaches 9.

Bundles
Limestone is not used in any bundles.

Recipes
Limestone is not used in any recipes.

Tailoring
Limestone is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth in the feed to create a  Shirt. It is a gray dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed.

Quests
1 Limestone may be requested by Bullhead, Largemouth Bass, or Midnight Carp in a Fish Pond quest to increase the capacity of the pond from 5 to 7.

History
1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.  Can be requested in Fish Pond quests. Can be produced by Fish Ponds.
