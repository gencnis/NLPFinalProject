Lobster Bisque is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit. It can also randomly be found at the Traveling Cart for 615-1,025g.

Bundles
Lobster Bisque is not used in any bundles.

Tailoring
Lobster Bisque is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create a Shirt. 

Quests
Lobster Bisque is not used in any quests.

Trivia
Receiving the letter from Willy with the Lobster Bisque recipe on 14 Winter, Year 2 and watching The Queen of Sauce before checking the mail will result in the letter still in the mailbox, but the recipe box at the bottom of the letter will be blank.
Although it is labelled as "A secret recipe", it can be learned from The Queen of Sauce.
History
1.3.27: The Lobster Bisque recipe now accepts Large Milk in addition to regular milk.
1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
1.5: Can now be prepared using a Cookout Kit.
