The Wind Spire is a dagger weapon that can be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on levels 20-39 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 3 to 1 and sell price lowered from 150g to 50g.
