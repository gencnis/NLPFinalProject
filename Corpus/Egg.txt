The Egg and the Brown Egg are animal products  obtained from White, Brown, or Blue Chickens.  Marnie may also gift you 12 eggs at the Feast of the Winter Star.  
Eggs can be hatched in an incubator, which comes with a Big or Deluxe Coop.

Bundles
The Egg may be substituted for the Large Egg in the  remixed Animal Bundle in the Pantry.
Ten of any type of egg are required for the  Home Cook's Bundle on the Bulletin Board (Remixed).

Tailoring
Egg is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Plain Overalls.  

Quests
The Egg may be randomly requested during any season at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 150g and 150 Friendship points.  An Egg will never be requested unless you own a White Chicken, Brown Chicken, or Duck.  (Owning a Duck will result in a chicken egg being requested.)
History
1.0: Introduced.
1.1: Increased value from 40g to 50g.
1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
