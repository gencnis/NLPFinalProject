Vinegar





An aged fermented liquid used in many cooking recipes.

Healing Effect:

Sell Price:

100g


Vinegar is a cooking ingredient that can be used in a variety of recipes. It can be purchased for 200g at Pierre's General Store. It is the only cooking ingredient that can't be made using Artisan equipment.

The Blue sash combined with an orange shirt may be a reference to one of the outfits worn in Dragon Ball Z.
