The Femur is a club weapon that is a possible reward for the chest on level 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game. It cannot be obtained in any other way.

History
1.5: Previously unobtainable weapon introduced.[1] Weapon level increased from 1 to 2, sell price from 50 to 100.
