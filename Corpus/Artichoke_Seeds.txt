Artichoke Seeds are a type of seed. Mature plants yield Artichokes.
They can be purchased at Pierre's General Store starting in year 2, from the Magic Shop Boat at the Night Market on Winter 17 (starting in year 2), and occasionally from the Traveling Cart. They can also be obtained by using the Seed Maker, as well as having a chance of being planted when using Mixed Seeds.  Five to twenty Artichoke Seeds may occasionally be found in treasure rooms in the Skull Cavern.
