Chef Hat





The traditional hat worn by a head chef.


Information


Source:

Abandoned House


Achievement:

Gourmet Chef


Achievement Description:

Cook every recipe.


Purchase Price:

1,000g


Sell Price:

Cannot be sold



The Chef Hat is a hat that can be purchased from the Abandoned House for 1,000g after earning the "Gourmet Chef" Achievement (cook every recipe).
