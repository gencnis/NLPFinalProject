Soggy Newspaper is a type of trash that can be caught when fishing during all seasons, in any location, using either a Fishing Pole or a Crab Pot. It can also be found in Garbage Cans, and any crab pot fish may produce Soggy Newspaper when placed in a Fish Pond.
Soggy Newspaper can be recycled into Torches or Cloth.
