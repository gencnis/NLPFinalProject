This page is for the item drop. For the monster from which this item drops, see Slimes.
Slime is a Monster Loot item which is dropped by Slimes. Killing a single Slime can drop up to two Slime. Slime can also be obtained from Slime Balls or purchased from Krobus for 10g on Mondays (up to 50 per Monday).  It may randomly appear in the Traveling Cart for 100-1,000g. A Slimejack Fish Pond may also produce 10, 30, or 50 Slime daily when the population reaches 9.
