The Topaz Ring is a ring that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 1,000g after completing the Initiation quest (kill 10 Slimes). It can also be found in Fishing Treasure Chests after reaching Fishing level 2.
The Topaz Ring increases weapon precision (decreases the chance that a strike will cause no damage) by 10% while equipped. 

Dyeing
Topaz Ring can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
