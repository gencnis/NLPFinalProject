The Elf Blade is an dagger weapon that is a possible reward for the chest on level 20 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game. It cannot be obtained in any other way.

History
1.5: Previously unobtainable weapon introduced. Level lowered from 3 to 2 and sell price lowered from 150g to 100g.
