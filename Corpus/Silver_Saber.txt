The Silver Saber is a sword weapon that can be purchased for 750g from the Adventurer's Guild after reaching level 20 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 50 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 3 to 2 and sell price lowered from 150g to 100g. Added as a possible reward on floor 50 in the remixed Mines.  Bug where weapon defense had no effect fixed.
