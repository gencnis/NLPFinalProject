The Wood Club is a club weapon that can be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on levels 1-39 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 20 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.
