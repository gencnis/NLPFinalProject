Rare Disc





A heavy black disc studded with peculiar red stones. When you hold it, you're overwhelmed with a feeling of dread.

The Rare Disc is an Artifact that can be obtained from:

dropped by a Bat (regular, frost, or lava)
dropped by a Shadow Brute
dropped by a Shadow Shaman
dropped by a Spider
inside a Fishing Treasure Chest
inside an Artifact Trove

Donation
Donation of this item contributes to the total count of donations at the Museum.  To earn the 'Burnt Offering' reward, you must donate a Rare Disc, a Dwarf Gadget, and 11+ artifacts.
