The Lava Katana is a sword weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 25,000g after reaching the bottom level of The Mines.

History
1.5: Level lowered from 15 to 10 and sell price lowered from 750g to 500g.  Bug where weapon defense had no effect fixed.
