The Bone Sword is a sword weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 6,000g after reaching level 75 in The Mines. It also has a 4% chance to be dropped from Skeletons. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 90 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.5: Skeletons now have 4% chance of dropping a Bone Sword. Level lowered from 10 to 5 and sell price lowered from 500g to 250g.
