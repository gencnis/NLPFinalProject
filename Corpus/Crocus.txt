The Crocus is a flower found via Foraging in Winter. It can be found in Pelican Town, the Railroad, the Bus Stop, the Mountains, Cindersap Forest, or the Backwoods. It can also be grown from Winter wild seeds.

Tailoring
Crocus is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create a Shirt.  It can be used in dyeing, serving as a purple dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests
The Crocus may be randomly requested in Winter at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 180g and 150 Friendship points.
