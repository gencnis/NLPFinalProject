Club Card








Information 


Source:

"The Mysterious Qi" Quest


Sell Price:

Cannot be sold


The Club Card is obtained after completing "The Mysterious Qi" Quest.  It is used to enter the Casino.
Once obtained, it can be found in the wallet in the The Player's Menu on the Skills tab.
