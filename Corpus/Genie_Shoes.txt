Genie Shoes are a footwear item in Stardew Valley. They can be obtained from Fishing Treasure Chests.  They may also be obtained by breaking crates and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on level 40+ in the Skull Cavern and in the Quarry Mine.
They have the second highest immunity buff in the game for footwear at +6, just behind the Mermaid Boots at +8 and just ahead of the Cinderclown Shoes at +5.
