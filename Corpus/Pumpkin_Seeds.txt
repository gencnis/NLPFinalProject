Pumpkin Seeds are a type of seed. Mature plants yield Pumpkins.
They can be purchased at Pierre's General Store, at JojaMart, from the Magic Shop Boat at the Night Market on Winter 17, and occasionally from the Traveling Cart. Nine seeds can be obtained from Gunther at the Museum after donating 35 items. They can also be obtained by using the Seed Maker, as well as having a chance of being planted when using Mixed Seeds.  Five to twenty Pumpkin Seeds may occasionally be found in treasure rooms in the Skull Cavern.
