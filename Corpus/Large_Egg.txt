The Large Egg and the Large Brown Egg are animal products obtained from Chickens with sufficient friendship. Large Eggs produce Quality Mayonnaise when used with a Mayonnaise Machine. For cooking, regular and large eggs may be used interchangeably.
Large Eggs can be hatched in an incubator, which comes with a Big or Deluxe Coop.
