Neptunite





A jet-black crystal that is unusually reflective.


Information


Source:

 Magma Geode Omni Geode Black Slime


Sell Price:

400g


 Gemologist Profession: (+30% Sell Price)

520g

Neptunite is a mineral that can be found in the Magma Geode and the Omni Geode.  It can also be dropped by Black Slimes.
