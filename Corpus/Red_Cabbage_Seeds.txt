Red Cabbage Seeds are a type of seed that can be found:

at Pierre's General Store for 100g starting in Year 2.
at the Magic Shop Boat at the Night Market on Winter 16 for 100g starting in Year 2.
occasionally at the Traveling Cart for 150-1,000g. If the "Guarantee Year 1 Completable" in the "Advanced Game Options" is checked, they are guaranteed to be available at the Traveling Cart at least once in Year 1.
in a Seed Maker from a processed Red Cabbage.
in the Skull Cavern from slain Mummies (0.2% chance).
in the Skull Cavern from slain Serpents (0.2% chance).
in the Skull Cavern from slain Purple Slimes (0.125% chance). [1]
occasionally in Skull Cavern treasure rooms (1/675 chance (≈0.15% chance) for 5 to 20 seeds).
Mature plants yield Red Cabbage.
