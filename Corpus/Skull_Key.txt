Skull Key








Information 


Source:

The Mines (Floor 120)


Sell Price:

Cannot be sold


The Skull Key is obtained on Floor 120 of The Mines.  It unlocks the door to the Skull Cavern in The Desert and the Junimo Kart arcade game in The Stardrop Saloon.
Once obtained, it can be found in the wallet in the The Player's Menu on the Skills tab.
