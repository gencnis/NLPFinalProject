Tent








Open Hours:

6am to 2am


Address:

The Mountain


Occupants:

 Linus


The Tent is home to Linus, who spends much of his day in and around the tent. It's located on the Mountain northeast of the Carpenter's Shop and east of the passage to the Railroad. It's the only bedroom you can enter without first gaining any friendship points.
