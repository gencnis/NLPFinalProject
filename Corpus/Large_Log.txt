Large Logs are fallen trees found on The Farm. A single large log is found in Cindersap Forest (blocking the entrance to the Secret Woods). Large Logs can also be found in the Mines (floors 41-69 with Shrine of Challenge activated). A steel Axe (or better quality) is required to remove a large log, yielding 8 Hardwood (10 with the Forester profession) and 25 Foraging Experience points. Large Logs will never reappear once removed.
