The Steel Falchion is a sword weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 9,000g after reaching level 90 in The Mines.  It can also be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on levels 40+ in the Skull Cavern. It is a possible reward for the chest on level 110 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History
1.0: Introduced.
1.4: Damage changed from 26-38 to 28-46. Level increased from 14 to 16, sell price increased from 700g to 800g.
1.5: Level lowered from 16 to 8 and sell price lowered from 800g to 400g.
