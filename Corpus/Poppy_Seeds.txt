Poppy Seeds are a type of seed. Mature plants yield Poppy flowers.
They can be purchased  at Pierre's General Store, at JojaMart, from the Magic Shop Boat at the Night Market on Winter 16, and occasionally from the Traveling Cart. They can also be obtained by using the Seed Maker.
