The Quality Sprinkler is a crafted item that waters the 8 adjacent tiles every morning (a 3x3 area). Only tilled tiles will be watered. The Quality Sprinkler is unlocked at Farming level 6, and is a reward for completing the  Summer Crops Bundle in the Pantry and the  Garden Bundle in the Pantry (Remixed). It can also randomly rotate into stock at the Traveling Cart for 1,350-2,250g.  Four Quality Sprinklers may occasionally be found in treasure rooms in the Skull Cavern.
Sprinklers cannot be used to water Garden Pots or the pet's water bowl. Sprinklers cannot be placed on sand.
