Fish Taco is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit. It can also randomly appear in Krobus' shop on Saturdays for 50g-500g, in the Stardrop Saloon's rotating stock for 1,000g, or at the Traveling Cart for 1,500-2,500g.

Bundles
Fish Taco is not used in any bundles.

Tailoring
Fish Taco is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the  Party Hat (green). It can be used in dyeing, serving as a yellow dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests
Fish Taco is not used in any quests.
