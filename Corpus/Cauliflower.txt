Tailoring
Cauliflower is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth in the feed to create a dyeable  Shirt And Belt. It is a white dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed.

Quests
Jodi will request a fresh cauliflower by mail on Spring 19th, for the "Jodi's Request" Quest.  The reward is 350g and 1 Friendship heart.
Cauliflower may be randomly requested in Spring at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 525g and 150 Friendship points.
History
1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
