The Savage Ring is a ring that can be obtained as a reward from Gil at the Adventurer's Guild after completing the Monster Eradication Goal of killing 150 Void Spirits (Shadow Shaman / Shadow Brutes). After that, it can be purchased from Marlon for 25,000g.
The Savage Ring gives the player an Adrenaline Rush after slaying a Monster.  This is a 3-second  Speed (+2) buff that stacks on top of any other Speed buffs that may be active.

Dyeing
Savage Ring can be used in dyeing, serving as a yellow dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

History
1.4: Removed bug which caused the speed buff to last longer by getting slimed while the buff was active.
