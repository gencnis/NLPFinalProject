The Kudgel is a club weapon that is a possible reward for the chest on level 80 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game. It is also a possible drop when breaking crates and barrels on levels 100 and lower.

History
1.5: Previously unobtainable weapon introduced as remixed mine reward and crates and barrels drop.
