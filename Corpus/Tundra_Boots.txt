Tundra Boots are a footwear item in Stardew Valley. They can be obtained from the chest at level 50 of The Mines, found in Fishing Treasure Chests after reaching Fishing level 2, or purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for 750g after reaching level 50 of The Mines.

History
1.4: Added the possibility to be purchased in the Adventurer's Guild after reaching level 50 of the Mines.
