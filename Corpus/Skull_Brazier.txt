The Skull Brazier is a crafted lighting item that can be placed indoors or outdoors.  Right-clicking on the brazier toggles the fire on and off. When toggled on, the brazier influences a radius of approximately 7 tiles with a moderate amount of light.
Brazier recipes must be purchased in order from the Carpenter's Shop.  Purchasing one brazier recipe unlocks the next. The shop interface must be closed and reopened, but the next recipe is available immediately.

 Wooden Brazier (250g)
 Stone Brazier (400g)
 Barrel Brazier (800g)
 Stump Brazier (800g)
 Gold Brazier (1,000g)
 Carved Brazier (2,000g)
 Skull Brazier (3,000g)
 Marble Brazier (5,000g)
In total, buying all recipes for braziers costs 13,250g. Buying all recipes up to and including the Skull Brazier costs 8,250g.

History
1.5: Changed recipe from 10 Hardwood, 1 Solar Essence, and 1 Coal to 10 Bone Fragments.
